ABOUT ME

I'm a former middle school history teacher turned coder. During the last 5 years, I taught middle school history to 7th and 8th graders. I also taught a computer science elective for two years. It was during this elective that I discovered how exciting web development was.

What I have enjoyed the most has been the ability to use hard skills to create algorithms. Seeing tangible results of what I created has been fulfilling. I have created different websites, applications, and games for younger audiences.

I am mastering my web development skills to be able to expand them into different industries.
